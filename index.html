The 5 Whys


In this section, you first start to think about some ongoing soical issues you are interested in. Then, you fill out an organinzer called the 5 Whys. First you name the issue. Then, you would says what are the causes of this issue. Then, you would say what are the causes of the new issue and so on and so forth until you can't think of any more causes and effects. Then, you will explore and research more on the topic you are interted in. Finally, you make a cause and effect diagram of your social issue.
